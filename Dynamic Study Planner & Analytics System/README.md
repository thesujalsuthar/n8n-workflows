# Study Planner Workflow

## Overview  
The Study Planner is an intelligent scheduling and analytics tool designed to optimize study sessions by adapting to learner progress, energy levels, and deadline urgency. It integrates with popular calendar applications and offers personalized notifications to enhance productivity and time management.

---

## Features

### Adaptive Scheduling  
- **Study Sessions:** Dynamically schedules study sessions based on user progress and priorities.  
- **Breaks:** Incorporates breaks strategically to maintain high energy and focus.  
- **Revision Timings:** Allocates revision sessions considering deadlines and learner understanding.  
- **Criteria Used:**  
  - Learner Progress  
  - Energy Levels  
  - Deadline Urgency  

### Integrations  
- Supports synchronization and updates with the following calendar apps:  
  - Google Calendar  
  - Apple Calendar  
  - Outlook Calendar  

### Notifications  
- **Personalized Reminders:** Sends targeted notifications tailored to individual schedules and study needs.

### Analytics  
- **Progress Tracking:** Monitors and reports learner progression across subjects and topics.  
- **Energy Level Analysis:** Evaluates energy patterns to optimize session scheduling.  
- **Deadline Approach Analysis:** Tracks impending deadlines to prioritize study tasks effectively.  

---

## User Data

### Learner Progress  
- Manages details on:  
  - Subjects  
  - Topics  
  - Completion Percentages per subject or topic  

### Energy Levels  
- Captures:  
  - Time-of-day energy variations  
  - Self-reported energy scores  

### Deadlines  
- Stores deadline information including:  
  - Subject  
  - Topic  
  - Deadline Date  
  - Urgency Level  

---

## Scheduling Engine

### Input Parameters  
- Learner Progress  
- Energy Levels  
- Deadline Urgency  

### Output  
- Generates:  
  - **Session Schedule:** A list of scheduled activities sized by time and type with details such as:  
    - Date and Time  
    - Session Type (study, break, revision)  
    - Subject  
    - Topic  
    - Duration (minutes)  

---

- Upcoming Deadline  
- Progress Check  

### Delivery Methods

This workflow facilitates a personalized study experience that adapts dynamically to user needs, ensures timely reminders, and provides comprehensive insights through data-driven analytics.
